Thor

Thor is a super hero that is the god of lightning and also has a near indestructable hammer.

Iron Man

Iron Man is a superhero named Tony Stark with lots of high tech stuff including his suit.

Spider Man

Spider Man is a teenager superhero that goes by the name of Peter Parker. He originally gets bit by a spider that changes him into having powers of webslinging and other things.

Ant Man

Ant Man is a normal person that gets a suit from a rich inventor. The suit allows him to shrink and grow as well as having the power to tak to ants.

Black Panther

Black Panther is a person that is part of a tribe like group with his family being the leaders. After his father dies the Black Panther has to take control of the tribe and fight for their survival.

DeadPool

Deadpool is a person by the name of Wade Wilson that works for the special forces. Later he gets injected by a serum that turns him superhuman even though he thought it was gonna cure his cancer.

Guardians of the Galaxy

The Guardians of the Galaxy are a group of intergalactical criminals that work together to stop soemone from taking over the universe.

Doctor Strange

Doctor Strange is a normal person that while working on healing both his phsical and spiritual state from a car crash ends up starting a quest that gets him into the mystical arts.
